Commonplace Book

2022–Present

Eunice Lee

Since 2022, I've been collecting quotes—words that resonate, challenge, or help me make sense of the world. The practice of keeping a commonplace book is one that has been around for centuries – from Renaissance writers to 17th century students. Many have used this tradition to hold onto words that matter, and in that same spirit, my commonplace book is an ongoing record of my thoughts over time.

And now, looking back, I realize that quitting, moving, starting over, again and again until I was happy is the greatest gift I've ever given myself.
